Payment to Beneficiaries

List of beneficiaries setup for the customer can be viewed here. Option to make a funds transfer from your account to any of your beneficiaries can be done using this screen. On clicking the Name hyperlink , you will be presented with a screen where you need to enter appropriate information and submit the same. You will then be presented with a confirmation screen to review the transaction details that you had entered earlier. You may then select the 'Confirm' option to send the transaction to the Bank.
