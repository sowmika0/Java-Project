Current Account (Preferred)

It is possible to have access to your savings, anywhere and anytime with the Overdraft Account [Current Account (Preferred)]. You can manage you overdraft account online without the need for more paperwork to keep.

- An automatic Overdraft of 250 USD (Default) with the option to modify (negotiate) the same at the arrangement level.
- Debit interest of Floating rate + 2 is charged as against Floating rate + 3.5 for other current accounts which are overdrawn.
