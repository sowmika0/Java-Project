How to use the T24 Portal

Nodes, Topics and Menus

Menus represent the top level organisation of the Table of Contents. In the screen shot below,  "User Guides"  is a menu. Menus replace the current display with its own contents. The main T24 Portal menu can be displayed by using the "T24 PORTAL" link in the banner

Nodes are the general folders that exist at the higher levels of the TOC and represent a logical grouping of topics. In the screen shot below, T24 Browser and T24 Connectivity are nodes and can be expanded and contracted. Individual articles such as "T24 Temenos Connector" within the T24 Connectivity node are called topics. Clicking a topic will display the item.
