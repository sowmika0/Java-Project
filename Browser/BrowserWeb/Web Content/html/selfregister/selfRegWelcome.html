What is Online Self Registration?

This is a new online feature which will allow the existing customers to register for Internet Banking service.

Where can I do the Online Self Registration?

Click on the "Proceed" button at the bottom of the screen.

Who can perform the Online Registration?

Existing Personal Customers who are yet to register for Internet Banking

What do you need to complete the online registration?

Customers who wish to register for Internet Banking via online need to have the following

1. Customer ID
2. A Valid mobile number/ phone number updated with the bank
3. A Valid email address
