Savings Account

It is possible to have access to your savings, anywhere and anytime with the Savings Account. You can manage you savings account online without the need for more paperwork to keep.
Also open a deposit account, allowing you to depositing some of your money for a specified period at an agreed interest rate lock allowing you to take advantage of high interest and the security of a guaranteed return.

- Normal product with eligibility criteria being- Customer above 18 years / Resident of GB.
- Credit interest - Floating rate for balances upto 25000 and +0.50 for higher balances
- Charges are applicable for specific no. of withdrawals (not a feature. If required will elaborate on charge conditions)
- Available in USD
